What We Clean

Complete Duct & Air System Services

Every service is performed by certified technicians using professional-grade equipment. No subcontractors. No shortcuts.

🌬️

Air Duct Cleaning

Full system cleaning — supply vents, return vents, main trunk lines — using high-powered HEPA vacuum equipment. Removes dust, allergens, pet dander, and debris built up over years.

🦠

Mold & Bacteria Treatment

EPA-registered antimicrobial fogging treatment applied throughout your duct system after cleaning. Kills mold spores and bacteria at the source, not just masks them.

🌿

Dryer Vent Cleaning

Clogged dryer vents are a leading cause of house fires. We clear the full vent line from the dryer to the exterior exhaust — longer dry times and fire risk eliminated.

🔧

AC Coil & Handler Cleaning

Dirty evaporator coils kill your AC efficiency. We clean the air handler unit, coil, and drain pan — restoring airflow and extending the life of your system.

🔍

Duct Inspection & Leak Sealing

Leaky ducts can waste 20–30% of your cooling. We inspect the full system, identify leaks, and seal them — lowering your FPL bill every month going forward.

🏗️

New Construction Duct Cleaning

Construction debris — drywall dust, wood particles, insulation fibers — gets trapped in new ductwork. One cleaning before move-in protects your family from day one.
